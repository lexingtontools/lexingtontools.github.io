Our Tool Wishlist

We had over 80 Lexington residents fill out our full interest survey last year. This information will help guide us with where to focus our efforts as we seek out new tool donations or make purchases.

The top categories we heard from you were: Garden/Lawn/Landscaping, Hand Tools, Saws, Dolly, Painting. Some of the additional items tresidents asked for specifically include: automotive, ladders, woodworking, moving, repair, sewing machines, roto-tillers, and cleaning/washing equipment.

Could you help donate some of these items?
