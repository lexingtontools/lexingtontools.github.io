Our Tool Wishlist

We had over 80 Lexington residents fill out our tool survey.

The top categories we heard you want: Garden/Lawn/Landscaping, Hand Tools, Saws, Dolly, Painting. Some of the additional items residents asked for specifically include: automotive, ladders, woodworking, moving, repair, sewing machines, roto-tillers, and cleaning/washing equipment.

Could you help donate some of these items? Please make sure:

  1. All tools are explicitly on our wishlist or you have coordinated with us via email or phone ahead of time. Anything new or in excellent condition, we will consider as an upgrade to our existing inventory. Contact us!
  2. All tools are relatively clean and in good working order. Please don't bring us your trash! We started a tool library because we also hate waste, but if something is past usable, please don't make us throw it away for you. This helps volunteers focus on getting the tools in the hands of members, not fixing and cleaning new donations.
  3. No donations include oils, expired consumables, or dangerous or flamable materials. Gasoline-powered equipment is not accepted at this time.
